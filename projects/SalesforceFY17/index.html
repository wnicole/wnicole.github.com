Salesforce Clubs FY17

Salesforce

Salesforce’s annual incentives program promises a trip of a lifetime to the top sellers in their company. The goal is to create a new, flashy and fun look/feel for the campaign that works over three different programs and for different locations. Dozens of different collateral pieces contributed to the whole campaign success. Collateral ranges from website and email blast images to booklets and custom die-cut room drop cards. I developed and art directed this year’s creative concept, which included incorporating unique, hand painted art assets into all of the deliverables.
