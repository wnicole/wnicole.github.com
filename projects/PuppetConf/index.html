PuppetConf

Puppet

Puppet wanted to take the opportunity to officially roll out their updated branding at the 2016 annual user conference. A primary design objective was to communicate their mission statement of “The shortest path to better software” by focusing solely on dynamic and engaging imagery without needing to use supportive typography. I developed a signage template system and dozens of unique and eye-catching oversized print deliverables that brought the new branding to life on-site in a uniquely challenging venue.
