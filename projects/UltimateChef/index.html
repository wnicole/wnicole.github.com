Ultimate Chef

Glu Mobile

I worked closely with the team at Glu to develop a logo and branding system for their mobile game: Ultimate Chef. In the game, players create and run their own fine dining restaurants. A logo package was requested that would allow the greatest degree of flexibility when using the branding within the game and on marketing materials, while still remaining cohesive. The final logo is fun and playful while making a nod at the fine dining component of the game.
