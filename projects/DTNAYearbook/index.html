DTNA Yearbooks

Daimler Trucks North America

Every year Daimler Trucks North America produces their version of an annual report that they affectionately call their “yearbook”. The book highlights the achievements from the previous year and all of the executives that helped make that year successful. This book would average 60 pages and had spot gloss, soft touch varnish, and foil embossing. I was the lead designer that owned this project from start to end and even went to press checks to ensure that every detail of the final printed piece was absolutely perfect.
