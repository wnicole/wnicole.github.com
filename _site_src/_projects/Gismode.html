Gismode is an art-nouveau inspired typeface made modern. While most art nouveau typefaces are overly decorative, Gismode has taken the most notable characteristics of art nouveau typography and simplified them for use in a more modern and sophisticated setting.

The name ‘Gismode’ is a combination of the name of one of Alphonse Mucha’s most recognizable art nouveau styled posters 'Gismonda' and ‘à la mode’, the French phrase for ‘in style’.
